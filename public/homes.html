Deposits and Fees

Deposit Amount:

Deposit will be held during the length of the lease. At the end of the lease a fee of
$100.00 will be used to cover carpet cleaning and deep cleaning. Additional amounts will be deducted
to cover damages done to the property. The remaining deposit will be refunded no later than 30 days
after the vacancy of the property.

Repairs and Maintenance

It is the tenants reponsiblity to keep the premises clean and sanitary. Tenants are responsible
to pay for any damage that occurs as a result of negligence or abuse.

Tenants will not make un authorized changes to property.

Tenants and Restrictions on occupancy

Authorized Occupants:

The only individuals allowed to occupy the property are the individuals on the Lease. No other
individuals well sleep longer than 1 consecutive night on the premises. No pets are allowed.

Sufficent Notice

In the case of needed repairs or attention of proprietor, notice of 24 hours will be given
to notify occupants that the proprietor or contractors will be arriving and entering the property.

Legal Behavior Notice

No individuals are to participate in illegal activities on the property. Any infraction will result
in eviction and prosecution. Tenants will forfeit deposit.

Health Notice

No smoking or vaping is allowed in structures on the property or within 25 feet of an entrance.
Infractions will result in eviction and forfeiture of security deposit.
